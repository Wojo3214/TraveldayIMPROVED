ATTRACTIONS

Giftun Island

I had a great time in Giftun Islands, one of the most popular place in Hurghada. I went snorkeling and scuba diving, and I can say the view of the million kinds of fish and corals is spectacular! Also, due to the hot weather, I enjoyed to stay and relax under the sun. I totally recommend this place, both for relaxing and adventures.

Mahmya Island

That was literally the best place for relaxation. I had really calm time while resting on beaches of Mahmya Islands watching sunset. The water is so blue and transparent! It's amazing!
