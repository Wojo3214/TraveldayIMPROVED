Contact & Support

Most frequented questions:

How do I change the language?

For changing the language, go to Settings >> Language and set the preffered one.

How do I find a destination?

You can go on Dashboard >> Top Travelday Destinations to see the most popular places or you can go Back to Website>>Find destination .

How can I make my journal public?

Go to My Journal. Below the name of your journals is an eye icon. By clicking it, you can make your journals public or unpublic.

How do I add a flight?

For adding a fight, go to My Booking >> Add a new travel, and then add the details of your next trip.

How can I edit a journal?

For editing one of your journals, go to My journal and click on the one you want to edit; then, on your journal page, you should see the Edit button below the title of your journal. Click on it and make all the changes you want.

How do I add an accommodation?

For adding an accommodation, go to My Booking>>Add a new travel, and then add the details of your next trip.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings >> Change password and type your old password, then choose a new one.

How do I edit my profile?

For editing profile, go to Settings >> Edit profile and edit your personal information you want to display.
